# One-Page PhD Interview Slide Notes

## Slide goal

In 30–60 seconds, communicate:

1. what problem you identified
2. what system you designed
3. what progress you already made
4. why it matters for research and labs

## Recommended structure

### Left side
One vertical illustrative schematic.

### Right side
Three concise sections:
- Lab Problems
- What We Built
- Why It Matters

## Suggested right-side text

### Lab Problems
- Inventory is updated late
- Experiment notes are fragmented
- Planning and booking are disconnected

### What We Built
- Messaging-native multi-agent framework
- Inventory + experiment memory + planning agents
- Event-centric memory and audit layer

### Why It Matters
- Lower operational friction in labs
- Better project visibility for PI
- Reusable benchmark and open-source framework

## Presentation message

This is not just a chatbot.  
It is an attempt to build a laboratory operations layer on top of real research communication.

# Nature-Style Figure Prompt

Create a clean, publication-style scientific graphical abstract in a Nature-inspired visual style, designed for a PhD interview slide. The layout should be a clear top-to-bottom workflow with strong visual hierarchy, minimal text, soft professional colors, and a polished vector illustration style.

Top section: depict a graduate student and a PI in a chemistry lab with subtle expressions of concern. Show three problem cues around them:
- inventory confusion
- fragmented experiment records
- disconnected scheduling

Middle section: place ChemClaw at the center, a professional cartoon lobster wearing a white lab coat, acting as the AI coordinator.

Bottom section: show three parallel ChemClaw branches handling:
1. inventory management
2. experiment memory construction
3. planning and booking

Final section: show a WhatsApp-style completion message being sent back to the student and PI, indicating:
- inventory updated
- progress organized
- booking confirmed

Style requirements:
- Nature-style scientific schematic
- polished vector illustration
- minimal text
- soft academic colors
- clear top-to-bottom logic
- presentation-ready
- not childish
- not photorealistic
